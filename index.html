PROFESSIONAL PATH

I am a strategic and quality-driven professional with over 12 years of experience in digital design, marketing communications, and residential property management. Recognised by colleagues and leadership for my hard-working nature and creative problem-solving, I bring a unique blend of visual storytelling and operational precision to every project.

As a designer, I’m passionate about crafting visually compelling assets that elevate brand identity and drive engagement. My background spans corporate, agency, and freelance environments, where I’ve led successful campaigns across print, digital, and interactive platforms. I’ve contributed to national and international marketing initiatives for brands such as The Bathla Group, MSC Cruises, Temperzone Australia, Snack Brands, Lander Toyota, and Snap Printing.

My expertise includes:
- Advertising campaigns, email marketing, and print media
- Website design, UI/UX, and digital product launches
- Social media graphics, digital advertising, and brand development
- Marketing strategy, execution, and performance analysis

In parallel, I’ve built a strong foundation in residential property management, specialising in tenant relations, rental strategy, and compliance with NSW tenancy laws. I manage digital platforms for property listings, coordinate open home schedules, and maintain clear communication with tenants and owners to ensure smooth operations and regulatory alignment.

Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Canva, WordPress, Google Analytics, and CRM platforms, I consistently deliver high-impact solutions that support business goals and enhance customer experience. I thrive in fast-paced environments, balancing multiple projects with creativity, consistency, and a solution-oriented mindset.

I enjoy connecting with new people and exploring fresh perspectives. If you're looking for a versatile professional who can bridge creative design with strategic property management, I’d love to hear from you.
